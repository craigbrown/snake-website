Privacy Policy

The developers of Oh No Snake try to collect as little data from you as possible - after all, you shouldn't need to tell us your life story to play our game.

However, in order to show you ads to support the game's development, we allow Google to collect some data via our app. You can find out more about Google's data collection here.

If you are in a country which requires it by law, we'll show you a dialog box the first time you open the app which gives you the option of declining your data being used for certain purposes, such as personalization of ads. You can change these preferences at any time by tapping on "PRIVACY" in the settings menu.
